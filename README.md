🚀 **From Paper Ledgers to Digital ERP: Engineering a Modern Workshop & Inventory Management System**🛠️📊

Many traditional workshops rely on manual register logs, creating critical operational bottlenecks: inventory leakage, fragmented expense tracking, and zero real-time financial visibility.

To bridge this gap between mechanical workflows and software engineering principles, my team and I designed and developed a comprehensive **Enterprise Workshop & Inventory Management System** tailored for **Ayaan Motors and Oil Lubricants**.
As the **Project Manager & Lead Database Architect**, I worked closely with the client to eliminate these inefficiencies by building a scalable, data-driven solution.

### 🔍 *The Bottlenecks We Identified:*
 * *Inventory Leakage:* Physical stock (lubricants, spare parts) couldn't be accurately cross-referenced with sales, leading to unaccounted financial losses.
 * *Financial Disconnect:* Fragmented payroll and day-to-day expenses made it impossible to compute a consolidated net profit in real-time.
 * *Zero Auditability:* Lack of automated tracking for who authorized specific jobs or inventory adjustments.
   
### 💡 *How We Solved It:*
We engineered a robust digital ecosystem built on a highly optimized, *14-table BCNF-normalized database schema* to eliminate data redundancy. The system introduces:
 * 📉 *Real-Time Financial Dashboard:* Instant breakdown of total income, expenses, inventory value, and net profit margins (Daily, Monthly, Yearly). 
 * 📋 *Digital Job Cards:* Seamless tracking of customer details, vehicles, and active garage workflows. 
 * 📦 *Automated Stock Management:* Master inventory cataloging with low-stock alerts and purchase trail logs. 
 * 🔒 *Enterprise-Grade Security:* Implemented Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and immutable forensic activity logs to ensure complete operational accountability.

### 🛠️ *Tech Stack Utilized:*
 * *Backend & Logic:* PHP 8.x (PDO Prepared Statements for strict SQL Injection prevention)
 * *Database Engine:* MySQL 8.x (InnoDB engine enforcing strict referential integrity & ACID compliance)
 * *Frontend:* HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Fetch API for dynamic updates)
   
This project successfully demonstrates how bridging theoretical database concepts with full-stack development can unlock immense operational value for traditional brick-and-mortar businesses.
